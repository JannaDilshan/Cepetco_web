CEYPETCO

The Ceylon Petroleum Corporation was set up as a state enterprise by Act.
No. 28 of 1961 in parliament and further amendments carried out subsequently.
The main objectives of Ceylon Petroleum Corporation are the following:
“To carry on business as an importer, exporter, seller, supplier and distributor
of Petroleum products. To carry on business of exploring for the exploiting, producing,
and refiningof Petroleum and to carry on any such business as may
be incidental or conducive to the attainment of the objectives”
